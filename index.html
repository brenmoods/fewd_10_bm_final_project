Macedonian tradition

King Cheese proudly make Bijano Cheese, a Macedonian style of cheese. It is hand made following a traditional Macedonian recipe and technique that has been passed down a long line of cheese makers. Bijano cheese is made in Melbourne using only the best locally sourced natural ingredients. King Cheese follow strict HACCP and Dairy Victoria regulations.

Enjoy a unique taste

Produced from cows milk, Bijano Cheese has a smooth texture with a slightly salty finish. Although airated the texture is firm. It perfectly complements traditional Macedonian sweet red wines.
