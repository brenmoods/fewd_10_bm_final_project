Tradition

Bijano Cheese is a traditional Macedonian style of cheese. The Bijano cheese is made with natural ingredients and follows strict HACCP and Dairy victoria regulations, and is locally produced in Australia. It is produced still using the traditional Macedonian recipe that has been passed on from generations. For more information on Bijano cheese feel free to contact us on
