Macedonian tradition

King Cheese proudly make Bieno Cheese, a Macedonian style of cheese. It is hand made following a traditional Macedonian recipe and technique that has been passed down a long line of cheese makers. Bieno cheese is made in Melbourne using only the best locally sourced natural ingredients. King Cheese follow strict HACCP and Dairy Victoria regulations.
